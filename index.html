About Stefanie

I studied at the University of Science and Arts of Oklahoma
and graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in 2004.
I have been working as a professional photographer since then.

I focus my work on weddings, babies, children, and pets.
I prefer shoots on the location of your choice so it's a
more comfortable experience for you and your family.
I am willing to meet you at your home, favorite park, or other venue
for photos that are customized to fit your style.
